Our Story

Discover the passion behind OpenMind Restaurant

Since 2010

Founded by Chef Michael Cooper, OpenMind Restaurant began as a small bistro with a big vision - to create a dining experience that challenges conventions while respecting culinary traditions.

Today, our restaurant has earned multiple Michelin stars and is recognized as one of the most innovative dining establishments in the country.
